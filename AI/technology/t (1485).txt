This presentation provides a concise overview of recent significant developments in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML).  We will explore the rapid advancements propelling these fields forward, focusing on key areas such as deep learning, reinforcement learning, and natural language processing.  Recent breakthroughs in deep learning architectures, particularly concerning transformer models and their application in large language models (LLMs), have yielded unprecedented capabilities in text generation, translation, and understanding.  Furthermore, the development of more efficient and robust reinforcement learning algorithms has enabled significant progress in robotics and game playing.  The presentation will also touch upon the increasing integration of AI and ML across diverse sectors, including healthcare, finance, and climate science, showcasing their transformative potential in addressing complex real-world problems.  Finally, we will briefly discuss the ethical considerations and societal implications arising from these powerful technologies, emphasizing the need for responsible AI development and deployment.  The aim is to provide attendees with a current understanding of the landscape of AI and ML and stimulate discussion about future directions.
