Artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) are rapidly transforming numerous sectors, yet their precise definitions and interrelationships often remain elusive.  This research paper aims to clarify these concepts and explore their synergistic potential. Machine learning, in contrast, is a more specific and currently achievable tool within this broader AI landscape. Deep learning, a subfield of ML, further refines this analogy, representing the dog learning not just a single trick, but a complex repertoire of behaviours through increasingly sophisticated training.
