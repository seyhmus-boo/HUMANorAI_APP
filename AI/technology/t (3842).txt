Post-quantum cryptography, a critical area of research, seeks to develop algorithms resistant to attacks from quantum computers, becoming essential for securing future blockchain implementations. While still nascent, the integration of quantum technologies promises to revolutionize blockchain security, scalability, and efficiency.
