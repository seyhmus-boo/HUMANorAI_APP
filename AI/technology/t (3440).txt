The vastly increased bandwidth and reduced latency offered by 5G facilitate the development and deployment of quantum technologies in several crucial ways. Firstly, the high data rates are essential for transmitting the vast amounts of data generated by quantum computers and sensors, overcoming a significant bottleneck in the field.
