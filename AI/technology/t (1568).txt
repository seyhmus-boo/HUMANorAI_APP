This technological leap, however, is exponentially more complex. However, this advancement is not without challenges. The ethical implications of autonomous decision-making during unavoidable accidents, similar to the trolley problem's philosophical dilemma, remain a significant hurdle. Ultimately, the successful integration of AVs hinges on addressing these complex challenges across technological, ethical, and societal domains.
