Mathematics, particularly statistics and linear algebra, underpins the development of ML models, with probabilistic reasoning and optimization techniques central to their functionality.  Furthermore, cognitive science and neuroscience inform the design of AI systems that mimic human cognitive processes, offering insights into learning, perception, and decision-making.
