This presentation explores the transformative potential of blockchain technology and its diverse applications beyond cryptocurrencies.  Blockchain's decentralized, transparent, and immutable ledger offers unique advantages across various sectors.  We will examine its core functionalities, including cryptographic hashing, consensus mechanisms, and smart contracts, highlighting their contributions to enhanced security and trust.  Practical applications will be discussed, focusing on supply chain management, where blockchain can improve traceability and combat counterfeiting; healthcare, facilitating secure and efficient data sharing for patient records; and digital identity management, providing individuals with greater control over their personal information.  Furthermore, we will briefly touch upon the challenges associated with blockchain adoption, such as scalability, regulatory uncertainty, and energy consumption. The presentation concludes by considering the ongoing evolution of blockchain technology and its potential to reshape industries in the coming years, emphasizing the need for interdisciplinary collaboration to realize its full potential and address its limitations.
