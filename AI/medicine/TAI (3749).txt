Telemedicine has rapidly emerged as a transformative force in modern healthcare delivery, significantly impacting access, efficiency, and patient outcomes. This presentation examines the evolving role of telemedicine in addressing contemporary healthcare challenges.  We will explore its diverse applications, ranging from remote patient monitoring and virtual consultations to telehealth-enabled specialist referrals and chronic disease management.  The presentation will analyze the impact of telemedicine on reducing healthcare disparities, particularly in underserved rural and remote communities, where access to in-person care is limited.  Further, we will discuss the technological advancements driving telemedicine innovation, including advancements in high-speed internet, mobile health applications, and artificial intelligence.  However, we will also address the associated challenges, such as regulatory hurdles, concerns about data security and privacy, and the potential for exacerbating existing digital divides. Finally, the presentation will offer insights into future directions for telemedicine research and implementation, focusing on strategies to maximize its benefits while mitigating potential risks and ensuring equitable access for all patients.
