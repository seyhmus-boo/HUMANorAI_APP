Research into CNT-based transistors and integrated circuits is progressing rapidly, aiming to surpass silicon-based technologies in speed and power efficiency.  Furthermore, their high thermal conductivity makes them ideal for thermal management applications, with ongoing studies exploring their use in advanced heat sinks and thermal interface materials.  In summary, the unique properties of CNTs, alongside recent improvements in synthesis and processing, position them as a cornerstone material for future technological innovations.
